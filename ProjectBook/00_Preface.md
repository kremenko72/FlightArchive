## Purpose

FlightArchive is a historical air traffic reconstruction project.

Its purpose is not to imitate FlightRadar24.

Its purpose is to reconstruct, as accurately as possible, the state of the world's air traffic at any moment in history.

Every architectural decision, every database table, every line of code and every feature must support that single goal.
